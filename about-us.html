About Us

SHAURYA ENTERPRISES is an young fast growing Export House in India operating from Ghaziabad. We are in the business of trading, manufacturing of Home Furnishing, Home décor, Garment and fashion accessories since last 10 years and have been exporting our high quality merchandise to our various customers in all over the Globe. The Factory is based in Noida/Ghaziabad manufacturing and Trading Home Furnishing & Fashion Accessories. We are also doing production for some good domestic brand.

It is our commitment to quality and timely deliveries which helped us make a reasonable growth over the years and also earned the privilege of working with a few prestigious clients. Under the Guidance of the Directors Mr. Rahul Kumar the Company has always kept itself abreast with the prevalent International Industrial & Working norms, thereby adhering to Industrial Safety norms, Merchandise Perfection norms, Norms pertaining to decent working conditions and Social Accountability norms.

Thus the Factory has successfully handled orders of various prestigious buyer from all over the globe as Kassatex(usa), Walmart (usa) & Sears(Canada), Kmart(Australia) Etna(Brazil), Bonami(Cz Republic) etc.
